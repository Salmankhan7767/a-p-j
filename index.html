A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and passed away on July 27, 2015. He played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He was known as the "People's President" and was widely regarded as a great visionary and inspirational figure.
